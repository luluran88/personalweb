Museum of Science and Industry Mobile Application Design

Overview

MSI seeks to customize the museum experience for parents and children. It aims to allow parents to purchase tickets, create itineraries and track visits as a way to plan future visits. It aims to allow parents and children to tour the museum with an interactive map and participate in an optional, museum-wide scavenger hunt. The goal is to make it simple for parents to plan a fun and organized visit and engage parents and children throughout each exhibit.

Interview and Observation

In order to understand the participants’ experiences of visiting museum, we conduct methods of observation on the MSI and interview. For the interview. we recruited 4 participants. All of them have visited a museum within the last year.

Questions asked included:
1. Can you tell me about the last time you planned a trip to the museum (or educational activity)?
2. Can you describe some of the highlights of planning and going to the museum?
3. Can you tell me about some of the difficulties you encountered while planning your trip?
